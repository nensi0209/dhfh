GLS History

Gujarat Law Society was established in 1927.Founded by Shri Ganesh Mavlankar and Sheth Shri Kasturbhai Lalbhai; it has earned for itself a place of pride in the field of education. The Society has had the benefit of the vast experience and vision of such pillars of modern Gujarat as Shri Chimanlal Setalvad, Shri H.V. Divetia, Shri Motilal Setalvad, Honorable Mr. Justice J.C.Shah, Honorable Mr. Justice P.N.Bhagwati, Shri K.R.Sant, I.M.Nanavati, Shri H.H.Bhagwati, Shri J.M.Thakore, and Shri Sudhir Nanavati, to name a few. These illustrious personalities have put the Society on a sound footing by their dynamism, sagacity and pragmatic approach. Today after more than 84 years, it manages around 38 renowned institutions which impart quality education in wide range of disciplines in its sprawling campuses and GLS Institute of Computer Applications is one of them. It has its own digital campus 'Zoomi website' which students access on their mobiles.
