FY Orientation

Orientation is an event organized to welcome the new students. On 23rd June, 2016, an orientation program was organized for Sem-I students of GLS University. The program started with prayer. Shri Balchandra Joshi, Executive Director, GLS University, heartily welcomed new batch and gave an encouraging speech. Shri. R. P. Soni, Campus Director, GLSICA, made the students aware about the significance of Computers and Information Technology in this fast emerging environment. Ms. Tripti Dodiya, I/c Director, GLSICA, inrtoduced all the faculties and gave a glimpse of the entire course structure of first year. Asst. Prof. Disha Shah gave the details about technical and non technical activities. Asst. Prof. Ankit Bhavsar gave the detail description about GLS CSI Student branch activities. University rankers of six semesters were given scholarship by GLS. Few rankers shared their experiences and encouraged the newcomers.
